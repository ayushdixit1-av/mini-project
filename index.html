RFI-042

What is the required clearance around UPS equipment for Tier III compliance?

Per TIA-942-B-2017 § 5.2.3.1, the minimum required clearances for UPS equipment are:

  • Front (operator access): 48 in (1,220 mm)
  • Rear: 36 in (915 mm)
  • Top: 36 in (915 mm) to overhead obstructions
  • Sides: 24 in (610 mm) from walls or adjacent equipment

Additionally, NFPA 70 (NEC) Article 110.26 applies — working space must be at least 30 in wide.

TIA-942-B-2017 § 5.2.3.1NEC 110.26Spec § 26 33 53 p.12
